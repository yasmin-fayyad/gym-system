Have Questions?

🏋️ What equipment do you have?

We offer state-of-the-art equipment including free weights, dumbbells, barbells, machines, cardio equipment (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes), functional training areas, and much more. All equipment is regularly maintained and sanitized.

👥 Do you offer personal training?

Yes! We have certified personal trainers available for all fitness levels. Sessions can be customized to your goals and schedule. Contact us for pricing and availability.

🏊 Are there locker rooms and showers?

Yes, we have clean and secure locker rooms with shower facilities available. Towels are provided free of charge. We also have a sauna and steam room in the Premium section.

🎫 Can I bring a guest?

Absolutely! Most memberships include guest passes. Basic members don't include guest passes, Standard members get 2 per month, and Premium members have unlimited guest passes.

🍎 Do you have nutrition guidance?

Yes! Standard and Premium members receive nutrition guidance. We also offer specialized nutrition consultations with our certified nutritionists for additional fees.

🧼 How clean is the gym?

We take cleanliness very seriously. Our gym is cleaned multiple times throughout the day. All equipment is sanitized regularly, and hand sanitizer stations are available throughout the facility.

💳 What are the payment options?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. We also offer flexible payment plans and corporate billing.
