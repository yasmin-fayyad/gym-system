Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! You can cancel anytime without penalties. We only require a 30-day notice for your final billing cycle.

Do you offer a free trial?

Absolutely! We offer 7-day free trials for the Standard and Premium plans. Just bring a valid ID and speak with our staff.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year at no extra cost. Contact our support team for details.

Are there any initiation fees?

No hidden fees! We occasionally offer deals with reduced or waived initiation fees. Ask about current promotions.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 5+ can get discounts up to 15%. Contact our corporate team for customized rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets. Flexible payment plans available.
